OUR MISSION

Principles: True People.

Everyone in the Rock Ride Team has made their passion into their profession. Rock Ride is a rider owned company, we are colleagues and friends, real mates who do their jobs with pride, dedication and idealism.

We are all individual character and entrepreneurs and everyone is a specialist in their field.

We have a personal, open and honest working atmosphere and appreciate the freedom that means for us.

In short: we live our dream.

Vision: True Products.

Our bikes are a viable alternative to the mainstream. They are innovative, progressive and individual. They deliver benchmark performance in design, quality and function.

With our products we want to achieve something for our customers. We invest a lot of love in many details and consider us a premium manufacturer with first-rate bikes and – we think – probably the best suspension kinematics in the world.

We aim to be independent, different and exclusive, we always want to stand out in the masses.

Mission: True Passion.

Rock Ride bikes are developed by passionate riders for passionate riders. They are not created in a laboratory or a marketing department but on the trails – that will never change!

Every year we aim to bring you new highlights in technology, design and quality in our segment, because we know exactly what a clever, innovative and exclusive product needs to deliver.

Enduro & E-Bike Fanes 27,5

The Fanes is a true enduro mountainbike that feels right at home on nearly every terrain. Touring, technical trail, light freeriding (even in your favorite bike park) or high alpine adventures are the right environments for the Fanes. Fun is guaranteed and you'll always have some reserves for the downhill part of your ride.

Because of the low center of gravity and the well-balanced kinematics or the frame you'll be hard pressed to believe that you are sitting on 170mm of travel on flat pedalling sections. Downhill you can really let her loose, your Fanes firmly grips the trail and gives you a lot of confidence. 26" and 27.5" wheels are possible, so you're always flexible.
